The Challenge: Potato Diseases in Agriculture
Early Blight and Late Blight are the most destructive potato diseases in South Asia.
Late Blight can destroy an entire crop in 2–3 days in wet conditions
Farmers lack access to expert agronomists
Timely disease identification is critical but unavailable
Current solutions are expensive and inaccessible in rural areas
4th largest food crop globally
15–20% annual crop loss due to diseases
10,000+ farmers in Nepal affected annually
